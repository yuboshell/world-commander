## Biography

Xirui (Dalen) Shi is a Ph.D. student in Computer Engineering at the University of
Alberta and the Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (Amii), 2024–2029, following a
B.E. in Artificial Intelligence from Sun Yat-sen University (2020–2024). His research interests
include continual reinforcement learning and embodied AI, with recent work on
vision-language-action (VLA) models, movement-primitive generation, and multi-robot
coordination.
